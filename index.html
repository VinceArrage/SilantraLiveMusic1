About Silantra

Where Elegance
Meets Rhythm

Silantra Live Music Entertainment is a boutique collective of professional musicians dedicated to transforming events into unforgettable experiences. Rooted in the vibrant cultural corridor of Montréal, Ottawa, and Gatineau, we bring the sophistication of jazz and the energy of pop to every stage we grace.

Whether it's the tender first dance at a wedding, the ambient pulse of a corporate cocktail, or the grandeur of a gala, our ensembles are meticulously curated to match the mood of your occasion.
